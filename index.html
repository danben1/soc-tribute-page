Kelly Slater

The greatest surfer of all time

Kelly Slater is considered by many to be unquestionably the greatest male surfer of all time. He started his career in 1990 and is still competing at the highest level to this day. He is the most succesful competitive surfer of all time with a list of titles that spans 4 decades and includes:
  • 11 World Titles
  • 55 career victories
  • 5 Pipe Masters
  • 3 Triple Crowns
Even after all this success Kelly still retains his competive desire and is about to commence his campaign for the 2023 Championship Tour at the ripe old age of 50, leading to one question on everyones lips - will Kelly Slater win one more world title before he retires? Have your say below.
